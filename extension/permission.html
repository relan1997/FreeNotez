One-time setup: microphone access

FreeNotez needs your microphone so your voice ends up in the recording. Tab audio alone captures everyone else but not you.

  1. Click Grant microphone access below.
  2. Chrome / Brave will show a permission prompt. Click Allow.
  3. Close this tab. You only need to do this once.
